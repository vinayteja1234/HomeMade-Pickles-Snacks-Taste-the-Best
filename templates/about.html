The Story of AuthenticFlavors

It all started with a simple family recipe for Mango Aavakaaya, passed down through three generations. For decades, our grandmother’s kitchen was the heart of our household, filled with the aroma of freshly roasted spices, cold-pressed oils, and sun-dried ingredients.

Today, AuthenticFlavors brings that very same warmth to your dining table. We believe that true Indian culinary magic lies in patience and purity. That’s why we never compromise on quality. Every jar of pickle and every packet of snack is handmade in small batches using 100% natural, locally-sourced ingredients with absolutely zero artificial preservatives.
